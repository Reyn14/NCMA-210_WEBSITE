About This Site

This website is made to help future nurses to learn more about Nursing Informatics. Everything here is written in a simple words so that everyone can follow and understand. The goal is to help everyone to see how important informatics in our job as modern nursing.

Nursing Informatics

It is using technology like computers in nursing

  • It record patient data
  • Can make fast the informations
  • Give us safe and high quality of care
  • It can support both nurses and patients

    • Patient-Centered Tools

    It is the resources that is designed to prioritized patient's needs (Health Teaching, Portals and Decision Making) it should easy to understand by the patient.

  • Electronic Health Records(EHR)-This stores the patient infos
  • Patients Portals- so that patients can see the lab results online and talk to the healthcare workers
  • Mobile Apps-This can also help the patient to track their health like the smartwatches

    • Innovation & Technology

    In this generation or modern nurses, they are now using smart tools to provide quality care for the patients.

  • Smart Beds- This is very useful now because it alerts nurses if the patients needs care
  • Wearable Devices- Becomes popular because it can track patients health status
  • AI (Artificial Intelligence)- Becomes part in healthcare because its more advanced and fast
  • Telehealth- Nurse can talk to the patients if theres a concerns
